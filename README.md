ALICO ENTERPRISES — Business Suite
> AI-assisted business management web application built for a real Pakistani SME client.
🌐 Live Demo: alico-enterprice.netlify.app
---
About This Project
A complete business management system developed for ALICO ENTERPRISES, a general order supplier based in Karachi, Pakistan. Built using AI-assisted development methodology (LLM-based) — converting real business requirements communicated in Roman Urdu into production-ready software.
This project demonstrates practical application of AI in solving real-world business problems for Pakistan’s SME sector.
---
Features
📋 Quotations — Create & send professional quotes
🚚 Delivery Challans — Track deliveries (Description/Unit/Qty only — no pricing)
🧾 Sales Tax Invoice — GST-compliant invoices (0–20%)
📄 Commercial Bill — Standard commercial billing
🛒 Purchase Orders — Supplier management with GRN
👥 Parties — Customer & supplier directory with NTN/STRN
📦 Products & Stock — Inventory with low-stock alerts
💰 Payments — Track received & paid out
💸 Expenses — Business expense tracking
📒 Customer Ledger — Full account statements
🏦 Bank Accounts — Multiple account management
📊 Reports — Sales, receivables, stock reports
📋 Audit Log — Every action tracked with timestamp
🔓 Role-Based Access — Admin & Viewer roles
📥 Backup & Restore — JSON export/import
💬 WhatsApp Share — Share documents instantly
🖋️ Stamp & Signature — Digital or physical signing

Reducing development time by over 80% compared to traditional methods
